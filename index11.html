STROLLING STRAXX RE-SPINS  

        
      
 

        
      
    
 

            If one or more reel is covered with the same symbol,creating
            a stack of symbols , Strolling Staxx Re-Spins are activated .

            During the Strolling Straxx Re-Spins all stacks of symbols and 
            connected symbols move one reel to the left on each spin 
            until all stacks disappear from the leftmost reel.

            If during the Strolling Straxx Re-Spins more stacks of symbols 
            appear on the reels,Strolling Straxx Re-Spins are re- 
            activated.

Bet line wins pay if in succession from the leftmost reel to the rightmost reel .Only the highest win per bet line is paid .Malfunction voids all pays and plays
